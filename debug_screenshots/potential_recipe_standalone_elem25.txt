My FAVOURITE 20 minute high protein recipe of all time

For the beef:
* 400-500g skirt/flank beef steak, trimmed
* 1 tsp light soy sauce
* 1 tsp dark soy sauce
* 1 teaspoon cornstarch
* 1 tsp bicarb soda
* 1 tsp sugar
* 1 Tbsp high-fry oil
¬†
For the stir fry:
* 2 Tbsp high-fry oil
* 200g beansprouts
* 1 bunch spring onions, chopped roughly
* 3 cloves garlic, chopped
* (Optional) 1 bunch tender-stem Broccoli (broccolini)
Then:
* 400-500g flat rice noodles

For the sauce:
* 1 Tbsp dark soy sauce
* 1 Tbsp light soy sauce
* 2 Tbsp oyster sauce
* 1 Tbsp sesame oil
* 1 tsp sugar
* 1 lemon juice

Method:
1. Cut thin slices of beef
2. Coat beef slices in ingredients and set aside for 10 minutes (or ideally 30 if you have time!)
3. Cut all your other ingredients to size
4. If you have dried rice noodles, cook them per packet instructions and rinse once cooked
5. Fry in a wok or a large fry pan on high heat the beef with a bit of high-fry oil
6. Set aside beef once browned
7. Add all other stir fry ingredients and cook for 2 minutes 
8. Add the noodles
9. Add all sauces to a bowl and then into the stir fry and coat the noodles until brown colour 
10. Add the beef back in and cook for 2 minutes
11. Serve hot & fresh
